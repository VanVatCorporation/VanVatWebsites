Discover the Power of Van Vat Tokens

Van Vat Corporation proudly introduces three unique tokens, each with its own mission to optimize user benefits and promote sustainable growth in the cryptocurrency world.

VANVAT

VANVAT is the CENTRALIZED token in the decentralized world, designed to maximize user benefits. We are currently running airdrop programs and rewarding users through this token, delivering real value and superior experience to the community.

VAVA

VAVA is a regular decentralized token, operating like many other tokens on the market. Although it currently lacks standout features, VAVA remains an important part of our cryptocurrency ecosystem, ready to grow and expand in the future.

FDVAVA

FDVAVA (Fully Decentralized VAVA) is a token fully based on user control, allowing them complete authority. Users can burn, create new tokens, or transfer tokens from others without permission. However, due to this nature, we do not recommend using FDVAVA as an investment method because actual ownership is not guaranteed.
